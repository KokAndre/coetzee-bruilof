Our Love Story

On August 31, 2019, fate brought André and Ellouise together, sparking a journey filled with love and unexpected joy. André, a bachelor at the time and Ellouise, a devoted single mother to two wonderful children, found in each other a connection that grew into something extraordinary. What began as a unexpected meeting soon deepened into a heartfelt bond...

On June 1, 2024, under a glowing sunset aboard a yacht, André made a moment to last a lifetime. Surrounded by the beauty of the sea and sky, he proposed to Ellouise, sealing their love with a ring and a promise of forever.

Now, as we continue to write our story together, we invite you to join us in celebrating the next chapter.
